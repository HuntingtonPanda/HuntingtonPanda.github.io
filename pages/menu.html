Ximapa

Menu

Carne Asada Tacos

A traditional Mexican dish composed of corn tortillas and marinated skirt steak that has been grilled and thinly sliced. Topped with cillantro and onions. Make sure to ask for red and/or green sauce as well as guacamole.

Breakfast Burrito

Breakfast burrito is a popular Mexian dish. It consists of a flour tortilla filled with scrambled eggs, cheese, bacon, potatoes, and salsa. The tortilla is warmed and then wrapped around the filling. It is a handheld meal that is both flavorful and convenient for on-the-go breakfast. A great way to start a day.

Enchilada Combo Plate

The Enchilada Combo Plate comes with 3 enchiladas, which are corn tortillas rolled around a filling of meat, cheese, beans, or vegetables, and then covered in a delicious chili sauce. The enchiladas are served with rice, beans, and a side of guacamole or sour cream. The meat filling can be chicken, beef, pork, or a combination of these.

Shrimp Plate

A plate of seasoned shrimp along with many complementing mexican side-dishes such as refried beans with cheese, rice with red sauce, salad, and guac.

Horchata

Horchata is a refreshing and creamy drink that is popular in Latin America, Spain, and some parts of the United States. It is made from ground almonds or rice, water, sugar, and cinnamon, and sometimes includes other ingredients like vanilla or lime zest. The mixture is blended and strained to create a smooth and creamy beverage that is served cold over ice. Horchata has a sweet and nutty flavor, with a hint of cinnamon which makes it perfect for those hot California days.
